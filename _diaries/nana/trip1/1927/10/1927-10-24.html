--- layout: title: date: 1927-10-24 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-01-20 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-october1927 originalDate: October 24-1927, originalTranslationDate: 20 --- Did not get up till 9.30 had a coup of coffee and off to Church with Sidney, Hattie. Heard a sort of a sermon more of a lecture on Encouraging Church in Politics. Robinson took us all to his home for dinner a wonderful reunion dinner all done by his delightful little wife Elsie. Then we went for a long ride to Lynn & etc. and home for tea at six.

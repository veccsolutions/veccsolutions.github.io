--- layout: title: date: 1927-10-25 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-01-20 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-october1927 originalDate: October 25th 1927 originalTranslationDate: --- Took the train to Boston alone. Spent two hours in the Public Library, the pictures of the "Holy Grail" held me spell bound went from one room to the other all full of interesting stopped on my way home and bought a tan sport coat to run about in. I found my fur coat too warm for this kind of weather.

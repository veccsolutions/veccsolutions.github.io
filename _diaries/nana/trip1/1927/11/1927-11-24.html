--- layout: title: date: 1927-11-24 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-01-31 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-november1927 originalDate: Nov 24 1927. originalTranslationDate: 31 --- Thanksgiving day, Never was there spent a more delightful one. Robin Elsie and the boy David came to dinner. Martha cooked the turkey and vegetables Elsie brought the pudding and Cranberries, and I mead the pie, mince and apple. We did not forget to say "grace" thanking Almighty God for all his goodness to us, then to bring to the end of a perfect day, came a telegram from the girls just at six oclock. I went to my room and wrote them, then said my prayers and to sleep.

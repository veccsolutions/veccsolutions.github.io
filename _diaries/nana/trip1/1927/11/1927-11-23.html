R. Southern in "General John Regan, a wonder he was indeed at the age of 68. I saw him 25 years ago in the same theatre "Hollis St" play Hamlet. Sad, he has to tody to the public and give up the classics, may be a relief for him simply because of the great contrast. Began to read "Jalna"

--- layout: title: date: 1927-11-03 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-01-25 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-november1927 originalDate: Nov 3 1927 originalTranslationDate: 25 --- Got up rather early for me as a rule I never stir till 8-30. had my bath and dressed at nine. went to the Nurses Registry to see about getting a case. They told me they could put me to work very soon. After lunch I went to Robinsons and Elsies spent the afternoon and had dinner, then they brought me home.

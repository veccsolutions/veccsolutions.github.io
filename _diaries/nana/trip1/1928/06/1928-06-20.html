--- layout: title: date: 1928-06-20 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-05-12 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-june1928 originalDate: June 20 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Arrived at San Francisco 8-45 Just in time for breakfast after which caught the 9-45 train for home. Ocourse I became very anxious to see them all. Was met at Glendale station by Harriett Jerome, and the dear children. Oh such a home coming, They all looked so well and prosperous. Good car & etc. drove me to their home 414 Niagara St Burbank, such a nice house with a room all to myself—I went to sleep full of thankfulness for one of the dearest daughters in the world, looking forward to seeing Lucy & family on the morrow—it was not her falt that they could not meet me.

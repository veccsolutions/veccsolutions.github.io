--- layout: title: date: 1928-06-18 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-05-11 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-june1928 originalDate: June 18 1928 originalTranslationDate: 11 --- Slept until late Flo & Claud called me to say good-bye to Claud, 7-30 had a good breadfast and washed out my cool dress then packed up, called up everyone to say Good bye. Then a taxi, where Flo, Louie went with me to the depot, there Violet joined us with a beautiful basket of fruit from them all. We got permission for them all to go thro the gates and they stayed till the train pulled out at 4-20 lovely of them dear old Friends. The sleeper was rather crowded but I settled myself down and slept five oclock. Had a trout dinner at six, then to bed.

--- layout: title: date: 1928-06-05 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-04-26 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-june1928 originalDate: June 5 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Began to toss about a bit in the night—did not trouble me in the least. And hardly stayed awake long enough to realize,--up eirly & out on deck as usual, our party of five are very congenial and enjoyed a game of bridge too, came in sight of land all excitement following the coast of Florida only one mile out in some places, could even see the automobiles on the highway near Miami. The line of land is rather flat and a sameness about it, was like our California coast, but peaceful at that.

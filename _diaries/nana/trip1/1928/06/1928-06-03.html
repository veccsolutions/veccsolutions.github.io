--- layout: title: date: 1928-06-03 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-04-25 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-june1928 originalDate: June 3rd 1928 originalTranslationDate: 25 --- Got out on deck by 5, AM after a good nights sleep. Just Heavenly, "Rocked in the cradle of the deep", just sleepy all the time not a care in the world. The Stewards had my salt bath ready at 6-30, then back to bed for another snooze, and up for breakfast!! All eat too much, dozed all day till night came, then bridge again.

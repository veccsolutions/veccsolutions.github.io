M. Said she was coming right down so just rested until she came—Oh yes, I washed a lot of stockings & things and pressed my dresses, then she came in about eleven and we just talked & talked, then we got dressed & went out down 16th to D & F. Oh!!‐what small country stores these do seem. Took my glasses to be mended & had lunch at Teholtzs tea room, then went up to the Denver after that took a car home with Flo, she has a nice little bungalow on Addams St, there she insisted on my resting till she got dinner. Claud came in 6-30, and we all had a good time. After dinner we walked up to Joy's saw them all there, then they put me on the car and to bed by 11 oclock.

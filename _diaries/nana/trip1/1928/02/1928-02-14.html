--- layout: title: date: 1928-02-14 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-03-15 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-february1928 originalDate: Feb 14. 1928 originalTranslationDate: 15 --- Sent cards to the girls and to Maud took the Jersey City Tube at 53rd Street & Broadway, arrived at Mrs Dwyer home #8 Lending Court. Found her very nice, comfortable home, arranged to go to her at 10 oclock tomorrow, care for a three months old baby at a hundred per month, there I took a buss right across Jersey City to the Ferry another way to Staton Island. Where I arranged to have my trunk shipped over to Jersey City

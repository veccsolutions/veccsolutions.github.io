--- layout: title: date: 1928-02-01 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-03-07 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-february1928 originalDate: Feb 1 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Started out to the Home Bureau where Dr Halsted gave me letters of introduction; saw Miss King who seemed to be impressed with me, she called a lady on Stratton Island, I talked to her she has a three month old baby she wants to turn over to me, she is to let me know tomorrow at ten. Was so pleased with the prospects I went to lunch at Millards 46th & Madison Ave terribly expensive but oh so good, and class, I should say so, bought two wash clothes and a hat on sale for one dollar, a ribbon to trim it with feel real dressed up, looked around at the pretty things in the Art Dpt. Oh!! And bought material for Dorothy's dress. Had Ice cream at Allice McDougles 57 St between 5th & 6th Ave. spent enough for one day so came home wrote to Harriett, beautiful weather again just like spring. Haven't a thing to read.

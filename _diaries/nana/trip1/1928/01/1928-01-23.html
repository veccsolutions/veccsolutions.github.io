--- layout: title: date: 1928-01-23 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-02-29 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-january1928 originalDate: Jan 23 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Up by 7-30 packed my grip down to breakfast, my last breakfast with Hattie & Sidney, called a cab, said good-bye, this brings to the end a most delightful visit, one that I shall ever remember, arrived in New York 4-20, phoned to Smith College only to find they could not let me have a room, but allowed me to keep my headquarters there, trunk & etc till hey did have one, so I went to the Martha Washington Hotel, am comfortable and more centrally located, but of course environment not so pleasant.

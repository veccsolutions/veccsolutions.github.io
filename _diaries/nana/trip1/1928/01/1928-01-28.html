--- layout: title: date: 1928-01-28 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-03-04 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-january1928 originalDate: Jan 28 1928 originalTranslationDate: 4 --- Had to go out for a good walk tho it was storming, blowing and snow, to the Bank I went and got the mail from "Smith's" College. Letters from Maud, Hattie and a good one from Martha, Finished embrodering the towel or rather scarf I hope to use in my dining room if I ever have one. Washed out and ironed my clothes. To bed eirly. Snowing & blowing

--- layout: title: date: 1928-01-22 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-02-29 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-january1928 originalDate: Jan 22 1928 originalTranslationDate: 29 --- Went to Christs Church heard Dr Singer, we all had a cozy breakfast, at home, went out for dinner, after which I took a long walk up Vasa hill. A gentleman at the boarding house told me about the walk, and would have liked to go with me, but I should have had to talk and could not have enjoyed the wonderful view and air half so much as I did when alone. I sat there for an hour, just gazing on the beautiful Hudson and Katskill Mountains. Cozy tea at home with Sidney & Hattie.

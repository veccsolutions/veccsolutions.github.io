--- layout: title: date: 1928-04-07 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-03-31 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-april1928 originalDate: April 7th 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Mrs Dwyre also gave me a ticket to the Grand Opera matinee could not accept it, as have made other plans. Met Blanch in the Grand Central Station had lunch, took a buss out Riverfront drive back by 5th Ave. had a good Dinner, chicken especial at a place on 33rd off 6th Ave. below 5th & 6th. Saw a very poor show, it served us right for going, got the train at 9-20 for Chappaque, they all met us at the station, had a refreshments and played bridge till 12 oclock.

--- layout: title: date: 1928-05-07 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-04-10 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip1-may1928 originalDate: May 7th 1928 originalTranslationDate: --- Got off 11 A.M. for the day in New York. First, I went to see about my return trip, engaged stateroom on the "Morris" leaving June 2nd. Bought a dark blue umbrella very nice one, at Sacks" 5th Ave, went to the bank, 2nd & 14th St. had lunch, a lot of small errands, large string of pearls restrung & etc. very tired so took the car back and went to the Car[xx]o Theatre, just around the corner form home. In by 10-30.

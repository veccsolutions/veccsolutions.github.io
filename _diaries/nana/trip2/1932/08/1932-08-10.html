--- layout: title: date: 1932-08-10 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-07-10 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip2-august1932 originalDate: August 10 1932 originalTranslationDate: --- Arrived Salt Lake 5‐30 am slept till 11‐am. Went to hear organ recital at Temple 12 noon then out to Salt Air. Had a dollar dinner after a swim in the lake‐was terribly disappointed in the lake. Poor excuse of a one. Dancing till 11‐Martha was kind enough to dance a waltz with me‐and I did enjoy it‐of course more than she did.

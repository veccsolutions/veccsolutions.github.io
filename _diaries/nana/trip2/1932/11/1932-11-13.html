--- layout: title: date: 1932-11-13 12:00:00-00:00 dateSuffix: categories: diaries,nana diary: nana translateDate: 2000-07-26 12:00:00-00:00 section: trip2-november1932 originalDate: Nov 13-1932 Sunday originalTranslationDate: 26 --- Archibald came and we went to "The little church around the corner." Just a block from the Hotel the Music was so beautiful I was so thankful to be able to go. Then A. and I went to Dinner, Childs Special all I can afford and only one a day, then to the Art Museum. Hurt my knee Saturday, very painful hard to get around but went just the same, came home had tea & toast then listened to the radio. At 9oclock Archibald went home he is a fine boy and I enjoyed [t]his day very much.
